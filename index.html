OUR COMPANY

INDUSTRIAL AND ENGINEERING COOPERATION LLC (IEC LLC) was established in Ukraine in 2011. The company provides global supplies of goods for mechanical engineering, our primary focus is components for railway rolling stock. We have built strong contacts with manufacturers, customers, and transport companies. The geography of our work is Ukraine, China, Kazakhstan, UAE, Poland, Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania, Czech Republic.

RAIL VEHICLE COMPONENTS

RAIL VEHICLE COMPONENTS

Our company supplies bogie elements, axles, wheels, steel products for rolling stock building and repair. We work with suppliers around the world who are competent in the production of components that are in demand in the market and offer competitive prices. An important part of our job is the examination of the compliance of products with both the technical requirements of the buyer and the requirements of the railway administrations of the recipient country. If necessary, we organize additional studies, tests, and certifications by authorized conformity assessment bodies. For deliveries, we find the optimal logistics solution in terms of price/terms, often non-standard. As a result of our work, our customer receives products with proven quality, and our prices and delivery times ensure the competitiveness of customers' products and services.

LOGISTICS SOLUTIONS

LOGISTICS SOLUTIONS

Based on the experience of successful tailor-made logistics solutions for our supplies of our goods, we offer our services in the field of intermodal transportation. We build an efficient supply chain, taking into account every detail, as support and control of the movement of goods and documents, the specifics of the goods to ensure their safety during reloading. Our logistics include delivery by railway and auto-transport, reloading of goods and stuffing of containers, and sea freight. We focus on emerging directions like Ukraine - Europe further through the ports of the Baltic, Adriatic, and Black Sea, and also The Trans-Caspian Corridor linking China and Kazakhstan with Europe.
